Business Skigit Logo

The monthly fee assigned to your account while at least one logo is uploaded into your profile page and available for promotion by other Skigit members.

  • When your company uploads new logo, a monthly fee will be assigned based on the current scheduled fee.
  • You must maintain at least one logo in your account, which will be assigned as you default logo.
  • You are allowed to upload as many as five logos in your acount all for one low monthly cost.
  • If we modify the monthly logo fee at any time, existing accounts will be charged the new monthly fee beginning from the 1st of the following month.
  • If we modify the monthly logo fee at any time, new accounts created aftert his date, will immediately be charged the new monthly fee from the day of account activation.

    • Business Skigit Maintenance

    The monthly fee assigned to each Skigit you create to promote your business and services.

  • At the time you post your business Skigit, you will be assigned a monthly fee for the month of posting. No fees will be prorated for the month so it's suggested to post your Skigit on the first of the month.
  • If you delete your Business Skigit you still pay the full fee for the current month. You will not be charged a fee for any month in which a Business Skigit is not active.
  • Any changes to Business Skigit fees, will be reflected only in new Business Skigits posted seubsequent to the fee change and will not apply to existing Skigit posts.
  • For all existing Business Skigits posted, changes to Business Skigit fees will be effective on the first of the month for all future fee changes.
  • For all new Business Skigits posted, the current fee will be applied on the day the Skigit is posted.
