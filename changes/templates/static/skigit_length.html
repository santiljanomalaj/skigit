5 and 10 seconds

Pros

30 seconds

Pros

  1. More time to tell the complete story of your awesome thing

  3. Good for humorous skigits
  4. 75-80% the recall of 60 second skigit

31 - 45 seconds

  1. Can be too long for some awesome things and there may be high risk of losing viewer attention
