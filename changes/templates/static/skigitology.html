Primary Skigit

A Primary skigit is a jet-setter, a leader, a ground-breaker …master of their domain! It’s a persuasive, charismatic skigit that others can’t wait to plug into and the source of every Superskigitt. The Primary skigit creates a lasting impression with the ability to grab viewers and not let go, no matter how hard the try to shake loose.

Plug-in Skigit

The Plug-in skigit is a way for you to connect with other skigits that depict a common thing, idea or theme. It’s a way for you to either start or become part of a group of like-minded skigits. The rule of thumb is, if you like it and endorse it… plug-in and join the group.

Superskigit

The amazing Superskigit! The Primary Skigit appears out of nowhere, suddenly plug-ins start to attach themselves and the Superskigit emerges, a potentially unending train of connected skigits with a Primary at the helm! … a bit much? We don’t think so.

Sperk Rewards

Do you like scavenger hunts? Earn awesome rewards just for uploading a skigit of your favorite thing and displaying the makers logo! When you see a logo displayed on a skigit thumbnail, click it to find out what that company is offering skigit creators. You can also click the Sperks icon in the header menu or Skigit upload page after selecting the maker to find out what brands are offering. It’s just another fun and unique way to enjoy skigit!

Home Page

When you first log in, you’ll land on the site home page. This is a collection of all skigits on the site, and it updates every time a new skigit is added. This is your home base so whenever you navigate away then want to find this page again, just click the Skigit logo to return home.

Following

Following people will enhance your Skigit experience by helping you stay close to the creators whose style and taste you best identify with. Discover the myriad of people that are creating skigits and start following those you like. It’s all about what you like, not just who you know, so if you come across a skigit you like, follow it’s creator and stay in touch with all of their latest works.

To follow a person, click the Follow logo in the Skigit viewing page to follow all of their current and future skigits.

Sharing Skigits

Don’t be selfish, Skigit offers an easy way to share your favorite skigits with anyone you choose. Sharing is an ideal way to tell you friends and family about skigits that they may not be aware of. It’s a great way to share the things you like with the people that mean the most to you. You’ll be able to locate this icon in every skigit thumbnail and also by clicking on the View icon in the skigit view popup window located underneath the video screen on the right-hand side.

View a Creator’s Skigits

Connecting with others is not always that easy. At Skigit, we give you the ability to view the work of your favorite creators simply by clicking on the View icon in the skigit view popup window. You find this icon located underneath the video screen on the right-hand side. Click on it and you’ll get to see all of the great works of art from the creators you love!

Add to Favorites

See a skigit you just can’t get enough of? Add it to your favorites page! You find this icon by clicking on the View icon in the skigit view popup window located underneath the video screen on the right-hand side. Click on it and you’ll never have to remember where you saw the skigit you fell in love with. From then on, just go to your My Favorites page through via the header drop down menu, My Favorites.

Posting Skigits to your Favorite Sites

Post to the max! We encourage you to post any skigit of yours or those that you like, on other sites for all the world to see. We will continuously update your ability to post to other sites so if we aren’t providing you that ability, we wanna hear from you.

Finding Skigits on Skigit

Search

To find skigits, just do a quick search in the search box and this is what may find:

  • A skigit thumbnail containing the word you typed in the search box in the skigit title.

If you entered the word “bugle” and bugle is not a word in the title of any skigit, you may will see

Apologies, but no results were found.

You can also locate skigits by browsing categories or locating your awesome thing within the listing on the Awesome Things tab found in the header menu on each page.

Awesome Things

Looking for ideas for your next family getaway but don’t have a destination in mind yet? The Awesome Things page lets you browse different category feeds like

  • Animals and Pet Things
  • Apparel, Clothing, Accessories and Fashion
  • DIY, Hobby and Crafts

These feeds automatically update as new skigits are created within the category of interest.

Your Skigit account

Profile allows you to get to your personal profile. Just click the drop down list in the upper right hand corner of the header of any page, next to your photo. Then click Profile and Settings to edit your name and profile information entered at the time of registration. From here, to change your username, display name or profile description, click the Member Profile button on your profile. Your display name is what shows up on your profile and on the skigits you create. Changing your profile picture is easy – click Edit Profile then click Change Picture to upload an image file from your computer. Other account settings such as Change Password, eNotifications, Delete Account and My Statistics can be accessed in the header of the Member Profile page.
